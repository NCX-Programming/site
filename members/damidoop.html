About Me

I'm Maple, although typically known as "DamiDoop" online. I mainly have been writing Kotlin and Java recently, but I also have some knowledge on Python. I also do graphic design occasionally. My first and biggest proper project is RNGTool-Android, which is a port of RNGTool to Android. Some of the graphic design work I've done includes the logo for libWiiPy and the icon for NUSGet.

Some of my interests include: Coding, primarily Kotlin and Java, though I'd like to learn Rust and HTML/CSS; Graphic Design, using GIMP or Inkscape; and Gaming, which I very often do in my spare time.
